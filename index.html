Meet Karin

Originally from Sweden, I've always been passionate about movement, wellbeing, and the balance between body and mind. It's this philosophy of life that naturally led me to Pilates.

After several years of practice and training, I discovered how deeply this method can transform the relationship we have with our own body. Pilates has taught me the importance of posture, breath, and deep strengthening — always while honouring each person's own rhythm.

Today, my aim is to share this approach with my students and to guide them through work that is both gentle and effective. My classical mat Pilates classes are designed to help each person feel stronger, more aligned, and more at ease in their own body.

Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned practitioner, you are welcome in a caring space where we take the time to listen to the body and progress at your own pace.

Move with more strength, balance, and control.

Originally from Sweden, I've always been passionate about movement and wellbeing. It's this passion that naturally led me to Pilates — a method that strengthens the body from deep within while improving posture, breath, and balance.

Today, I share this practice through sessions open to everyone, in a caring and encouraging atmosphere.

My classes take place outdoors, under the Valencia sun, so you can draw on the energy of nature while caring for both body and mind.

I teach classical mat Pilates, inspired by the traditional method with its emphasis on posture, breath, and deep-core strengthening. The practice improves flexibility, balance, and tone while working the body gently.

Sessions are held outdoors, under the Valencia sun, in a relaxed and welcoming setting. Practising outside lets you breathe fully, reconnect with the body, and enjoy a moment of wellbeing in nature.

Classes are suited to all levels, whether you're a complete beginner or already familiar with Pilates.
